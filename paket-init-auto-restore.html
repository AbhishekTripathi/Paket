Paket

paket init-auto-restore

Enables automatic package restore in Visual Studio. Under the hood, the command creates .paket directory, downloads paket.targets and paket.bootstrapper.exe, and finally adds import statement for paket.targets to all projects under the working directory.

$ paket init-auto-restore
